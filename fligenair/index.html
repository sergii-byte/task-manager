ABOUT US

Trusted Aviation Trading Partner

Fligen Aircrafts Trading Ltd. is headquartered in Abu Dhabi, specializing in the wholesale trading of aircraft and aviation engines. With deep industry expertise and a strategic location in the heart of the Middle East, we connect buyers and sellers across the global aviation market.

Our core strength lies in sourcing and supplying aviation engines, airframes, and certified spare parts. We serve airlines, MRO facilities, leasing companies, agencies and operators worldwide, ensuring every transaction meets the highest standards of quality and compliance.
