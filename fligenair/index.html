ABOUT US

Trusted Aviation Trading Partner

Fligen Aircrafts Trading Ltd. — Abu Dhabi, UAE

With deep industry expertise and a strategic location in the heart of the Middle East, we connect buyers and sellers across the global aviation market.

Our core strength lies in sourcing and supplying aviation engines, airframes, and certified spare parts. We serve operators, charter companies, leasing firms, agencies and private owners worldwide, ensuring every transaction meets the highest standards of quality and compliance.

We build long-term partnerships with our clients, offering flexible procurement solutions and efficient logistics to ensure timely delivery wherever it is needed.

With a strong focus on innovation, we actively explore opportunities in next-generation aviation technologies — from electric propulsion and eVTOL platforms to sustainable aviation solutions that are shaping the future of the industry.
